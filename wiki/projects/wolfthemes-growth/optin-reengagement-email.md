# Opt-In / Re-Permission Email — imported Ticksy contacts

For the **secondary audience**: past customers imported from the Ticksy export who never opted into marketing email. GDPR-safe entry point (Constantin is in France) — its only job is to earn a **fresh, explicit opt-in** before any promo. Soft, low-pressure, no hard sell. Send *before* the launch promo so the launch goes only to people who said yes. See [[launch-newsletter]] and [[audience-building-strategy]].

**Do not** add these contacts straight to the launch-promo segment. Only those who click/confirm here move into the marketing list.

---

## Subject line options

1. Your WolfThemes themes have a home now
2. A quick hello from the person behind your theme
3. Want to hear what's next for WolfThemes?

**Preheader:** No spam — just one question, and you decide.

---

## Body

Hi {First Name},

A while back you bought one of my WordPress themes — or reached out for a hand setting one up. I'm Constantin, the person who designs and builds them. Thank you for that, genuinely.

I'm writing for one reason: after 14 years, WolfThemes finally has its own store, and I'd love to keep the people who've supported me in the loop — new themes, updates, the occasional tip, and launch news.

But I'll only do that if you want me to. No assumptions.

**If you'd like to hear from me, just click below to opt in:**

👉 **[Yes, keep me in the loop →]**

That's it. If this isn't for you, do nothing and you won't hear from me again — no hard feelings.

Thanks for being part of the WolfThemes story.

— Constantin
Founder, WolfThemes

*You're receiving this because you previously purchased a WolfThemes theme or contacted support. We won't add you to any marketing list unless you opt in above.*

- The opt-in link should point to a confirmed-subscribe / signup form, not the store. Conversion goal = consent, not a sale.
