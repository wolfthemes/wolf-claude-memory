**Fill in before sending:** `[DEADLINE]` — a real, short window (3–5 days max). Add UTM tags to the store link so GA4 attributes the traffic ([[analytics-marketing-stack]]).

**wolfthemes.com is live** — my own store, where you buy directly from the person who actually designs and codes every theme. That means better pricing, faster support straight from me, and updates that don't get lost in a queue.

Whether you've been waiting to start a new site or you want to see what I've been building lately, this is the moment.

👉 **[Browse the store and claim your 20% off →]**

Thank you for being on this list — some of you have been here a very long time. This launch is the start of doing things properly, on my own terms, and I'm glad you're here for it.

— Constantin
Founder, WolfThemes
