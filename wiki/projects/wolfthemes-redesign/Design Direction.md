
## Craft-Inspired, Professionally Executed

WolfThemes should not feel like a generic SaaS company or a faceless theme marketplace.

The visual direction combines:

- Clean structure
    
- Editorial layouts
    
- Strong typography
    
- Generous whitespace
    
- Subtle handcrafted elements
    

The goal is to communicate professionalism and trust while retaining a human and creative personality.

Possible visual cues:

- Brush strokes
    
- Doodles
    
- Hand-drawn accents
    
- Organic imperfections
    
- Subtle textures
    

These elements should remain restrained and intentional. The overall experience must stay clean, modern and easy to use.

---

## Human-Centered Positioning

WolfThemes is not a software company.

WolfThemes is a one-person agency with over a decade of experience designing and building WordPress themes.

The site should make visitors feel that there is a real person behind the products.

The goal is not to appear bigger than reality.

The goal is to communicate:

- Expertise
    
- Craftsmanship
    
- Consistency
    
- Long-term commitment
    

---

## Positioning Statement

Customers are not only buying a theme.

They are benefiting from years of experience building WordPress products for real businesses, artists, musicians and agencies.

The person designing the products is also the person supporting them, maintaining them and improving them.

This direct connection is a competitive advantage and should be visible throughout the site.

---

## Avoid

- Generic ThemeForest presentation
    
- Corporate SaaS aesthetics
    
- Artificial "big company" messaging
    
- Excessive feature-focused marketing
    
- Overly decorative design
